The Habit Of A Smile

This is a fun post about smiling. Why fun? Well let’s face it, when we’re smiling we are normally having a pretty good time!

If you were to ask someone who knows me to describe who I am, undoubtedly one of the first things that they will say is that I am a continually happy or ‘smiley’ person. This is something that I always laugh about as it has been mentioned many times throughout my life! The good thing is I’m not complaining as a smile has a tonne of benefits to your overall wellbeing. Smiling is number 2 on the list of the 43 Habits of Absolutely Happy People. If you can turn smiling into a habit, then you can turn happiness into a habit.

Have you ever been in a sour mood and then someone has come along with a huge smile, some laughter or in a really good mood? Perhaps a baby that just looked up at you and smiled? How did this make you feel? When the person you are talking to or the people that surround you are smiling, you won’t be able to help but smile!

A smile has special powers. You can calm fear, insecurity, hurt and anxiety not only in yourself, but in those that are experiencing those feelings. The next time someone is feeling sad, scared, nervous, whatever it may be, smile with them and see how this makes them feel!

EXPECTATION OF A GIRL!



EXPECTATION OF A GIRL!

The life we girls live have never been easy for us. Right from the time we born we are also taught to rather accept things than to expect. But , the human nature cannot be changed be it a girl or a boy. As a girl , I have many expectations from life. From the day I was born , I had the expectation of being loved always. I loved all the attention as a kid and still love it! When I became a little older I expected my life to be a full time games period. I loved to play and play. As I became a teenager, the expectations increased a little . I always wanted to look good all the time, want to go on adventure trips and what not. I expected my friends to stick around all the time.I expected life to be like the Bollywood-movie like. I had a dream to become an Event planner, but as being a girl, rather than expecting support, i had to accept that it wont work-out.! And by the passing time , I kind of realised that many of those dreams wont really work out. !

As a girl , I dont expect my life to be without any difficulties but I expect that I should have strength at all the difficult times . I do not expected life to be candy sweet but I expect random surprises which would be welcomed. As a girl, I expect that all the choices I make in life should make my parents proud. Furthermore , I expect my life partner to respect and adore my feelings towards everything. I expect that I can raise citizens to can make our country proud. Being a girl , I expect to be respected by all the men and be seen as a equal. The expectation to see around enough places that I can have enough stories to tell . The expectation to be a sucessfull person and even if not the expectation to feel confident and Love myself above all .
