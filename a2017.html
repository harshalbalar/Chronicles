Letter of Editor

“The struggle you're in today is developing the strength you need for tomorrow”

Dear Readers,

Greetings! We bring you the magazine of Babu Madhav Institute of Information Technology's - “BMIIT-Chronicles”. We have tried our best to make this memoir to reminisce your eventful journey in BMIIT. BMIIT-Chronicles is a compilation of creativity and hard work of all the students who are directly or indirectly associated to it.This issue- Spring'17 is based on the theme “Steps towards humanity”.

“We make a living by what we get. But we make a life by what we give”-Winston Churchill

Spring'17 is our little effort to make you motivated towards the humans whom we can help. Those innocent lives who just need your little time and effort which makes their day,to fulfill our role as humans and doing our part to make the peoples a little happier.May it be your grandparents, a beggar's child, a hungry man or a maid working at your home;you just have to be little considerate about them and you'll see their eyes shine with happiness. Isn't it rightly said, “A flower makes no garland”; this magazine is a joint effort of the editorial team, our teachers and students of BMIIT.

We wish you to make the best from your journey in BMIIT.

We will see you again in the next issue – Fall'17

Rana’s Article

What is Deep Learning?

Well, Humans are self learning animals as this thought also comes to computer era that what happened if computer can learn itself. So basically Deep learning is concept in which, we teach something to computer and after computer automatically works and improve its knowledge .To understand What is Deep Learning, Lets Take an example,

There is problem in parking lot where 2 wheeler and 4 wheeler parking is different, so in this scenario we need some solution to solve this problem.

However if our computer knows that whether it is two wheeler or four wheeler vehicle then it automatically separate vehicles. To Accomplish we give some knowledge about 2 wheeler and 4 wheeler than computer automatically works on it.

There are two phases in which Classification occurs.1 – Training Phase 2 – Predication Phase

In Training Phase, we tell computer about object by images, labels, and other objective information.

Machine use this information to train itself.

In Predication Phase, we give some new object to computer to identify object.Computer uses its knowledge of previous training to identify the object.So basically it is quite difficult for new comer to computer era which has no knowledge about it. Don't worry, Biggest Search Engine Google Develop one api called Tensorflow to simplify this process.

What is Tensorflow?? What it can Do !!

Tensorflow is An Open Source Library for Machine Intelligence. It contains lots modules which help you to make machine self learned. It is Available For Java, Python, C++Now Think, How Google Image Search Works???Simply Google Shows lots of new images to its servers. Machine extract information about images such as rgb code, label, resolution, graphs and train itself with previousknowledge. When user upload image to find similar images, It extract same info try to best match with predictions.

Then servers make a list of possible output which nearly matched with given images.
